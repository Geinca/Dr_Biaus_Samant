Dr. Biaus Samanta

Dr. Biaus Samanta is a highly skilled and experienced cardiologist dedicated to providing comprehensive cardiac care. With expertise in diagnosing and treating a wide range of heart conditions, Dr. Samanta specializes in preventive cardiology, interventional cardiology, and heart disease management. With years of clinical experience, Dr. Samanta is committed to utilizing advanced diagnostic techniques and state-of-the-art procedures to deliver the best possible outcomes for his patients.

Dr. Samanta completed his MBBS from Medical College, Kolkata, laying a strong foundation in medical sciences. He further pursued an MD in General Medicine from the same institution, gaining in-depth knowledge and expertise in internal medicine. His passion for cardiology led him to obtain a DM in Cardiology from NRS Medical College and Hospital, where he honed his skills in advanced cardiac care and interventional procedures. With a dedication to patient-centered treatment and a commitment to medical excellence, Dr. Samanta continues to provide compassionate and cutting-edge cardiovascular care to his patients.
